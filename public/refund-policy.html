Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

⚠️ UNBOXING VIDEO IS MANDATORY

For any damage or return claims, an unboxing video is required. Without proper video evidence, we cannot process refunds or replacements.

Refund Eligibility

You Can Request a Refund If:

  • Product arrives damaged or defective
  • Wrong item shipped (different from what you ordered)
  • Product doesn't match description
  • Package never arrived (lost in transit)

Not Eligible for Refund:

  • Change of mind or buyer's remorse
  • Product doesn't fit (unless defective)
  • Minor cosmetic imperfections not affecting function
  • Products used, altered, or damaged after delivery
  • No unboxing video provided for damage claims

Return Shipping

  • We provide prepaid return labels for defective items
  • Customer pays return shipping for change of mind
  • Return within 3 days of approval

Replacements

When We Offer Replacements:

  • Defective product (if same item is in stock)
  • Wrong item shipped
  • Damaged during shipping

Replacement Process:

  1. Submit unboxing video via email
  2. We approve within 48 hours
  3. We ship replacement immediately
  4. You return defective item (prepaid label provided)

Note: Replacements are subject to stock availability. If out of stock, we'll issue a refund.
